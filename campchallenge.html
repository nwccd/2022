Camp Challenge
Each cabin creates one maze with two holes. A player has to get a ball through a maze and into the hole.
Construction Rules
Maze is 28 inches by 48 inches.
Short side is the starting side. Opposite short side has two holes.
The spaces between the walls in the maze are wide enough for the balls to move.
Walls are between 4 inches and 6 inches high. No exceptions.
Materials
Cardboard and tape is provided to each cabin.
Judging Rules
The "players" will be NWCCD Cabin Counselors of the opposite gender. For example, HS Girls Counselors will be the "players" for the HS Boys' maze.
Points are deducted for not properly constructing the maze. A "blueprint" judge will evaluate your maze for following the constructing rules.
Points are given for the most creative maze.
Points are given for forcing "players" to take the longest route to a hole.
