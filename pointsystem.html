Point System Rules
What is it?

There is a maze that each cabin will participate in.

Each point will be worth one roll of die.

The roll of die will determine how many moves their cabin moves in the maze.

Whichever cabin moves closest to the end (number of steps away) wins.

But… the maze is covered with the "fog" (cardboard). To get more vision of the maze, cabins must pay with three points for each “shift”.

The fog will be smaller each day, becoming more narrow so more of the maze is revealed.

The girls will start on one side of the maze and the boys will start on the other side.

Each side will war to push the “fog” to the other side. This is done by sportsmanship points and such.

How to collect or lose points

Win nightly challenge versus opposing gender cabin

Memory verses (entire cabin must recite the verse to a DPT member)

Cabin Inspections. Each day, starting Monday and ending on Thursday. Cabins that are clean and have the most creative design will be awarded. Messy cabins will be penalized by losing the "fog of war", having the fog moved towards them. This means that all girls must work together against all boys (and vice versa) to avoid messy cabins.

Games throughout the day will have opportunities for cabins to collect points.

Do crafts to collect more points.

Set up the cafeteria and clean up after meals to collect more points. Your cabin should be on time and do quality work with a good attitude.

Collecting the most water balloon debris will award points.

Good sportsmanship acts and kind acts will also be awarded.

Poor sportsmanship acts and being late will result in losing points.

Elementary campers will have opportunities to collect points during class time.

The Winning Cabin for each Grade Level (Elementary, Middle and High School)

The team that has the least amount of moves away from the finish zone wins.

Least overall amount of moves for boy and girl cabins will be counted as well before water fest on Thursday (totaled during lunch).

There will be one cabin winner per Grade Level. There will also be an overall winner.

The winner will be awarded whip cream plates to dump onto a staff of choice.

The leading team of gender war will receive additional balloons.
