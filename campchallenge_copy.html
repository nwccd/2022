Goal for Egg Drop
Your goal is to build a structure (with the materials that we give you) that will protect the egg from breaking.
Rules for Egg Drop
1) You will be given an assortment of materials. Each age group gets the same materials.
2) You may only use what we give.
3) 11 feet vertical drop for the egg drop.
Judging Criteria for Egg Drop
There are 3 ways to score: Best Design; Best Protection; Strongest Structure
Best Design - Structure looks awesome, is neatly put together, etc.
Best Protection - Egg is unharmed.
Strongest Structure - Structure can be dropped multiple times without breaking apart or has minimal breakdown.
